ggplot(candy,aes(winpercent,reorder(rownames(candy),winpercent)))+
  geom_bar(stat="identity")+

my_cols=rep("black", nrow(candy))

my_cols[as.logical(candy$bar)] = "brown"
my_cols[as.logical(candy$fruity)] = "pink"

ggplot(candy) + 
  aes(winpercent, reorder(rownames(candy),winpercent)) +

Q24. What original variables are picked up strongly by PC1 in the positive direction? Do these make sense to you?

Fruity is the strongest positive variable in PC1. Other positive variables that are not as strong are hard and pluribus. This means that these variables correlate together. As PC1 value goes up (further along the x axis), these variables increase.
